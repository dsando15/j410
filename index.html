Cal Poly Intramural Sports

Intramural sports can be found on college campuses all across the nation, including here at Cal Poly. Through Cal Poly ASI, the school offers a wide variety of sports for students to partake in, either in a competitive or recreational environment.

However, it isn't all about competition and winning or losing. These intramural sports allow for students to express themselves, meet people who have similar interests, and create closer bonds through the love of sports and physical activity. This website is all about that: how intramural sports impact Cal Poly students.

Sunday Night Softball

Sunday Night Softball

Sunday nights signify softball night for intramural sports, as softball games run from 6:30 to 9:30 on three separate fields at the Cal Poly Sports Complex. The games range from Division III, the most recreational division, to Division I, the most competitive league in intramurals. Every game being played on the same night creates a fun and inviting environment for both players and fans looking to end their weekends on a high note.
